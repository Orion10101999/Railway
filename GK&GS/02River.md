## Kolkata Bandargah :-


The Kolkata Bandargah, also known as the Port of Kolkata, is the only riverine major port in India and the oldest operating port in the country ¹. It is located in the city of Kolkata, West Bengal, around 203 kilometers from the sea ¹. The port has two distinct dock systems – Kolkata Dock at Kolkata and a deep water dock at Haldia Dock Complex, Haldia ¹.

Key Features of the Port of Kolkata:
- Location: Kolkata, West Bengal, India ¹
- Oldest operating port in India ¹
- Two distinct dock systems: Kolkata Dock and Haldia Dock Complex ¹
- Major exports: Iron ore, leather, cotton textiles ¹
- Major imports: Wheat, raw cotton, machinery, iron & steel ¹

History of the Port of Kolkata:
The port was constructed by the British East India Company and has a long history dating back to the 16th century ¹. It was the premier port in British India during the 19th century and played a significant role in the country's trade and commerce ¹. After India's independence, the port's importance decreased due to various factors, but it has since been revived and is now a major port in the country ¹.
